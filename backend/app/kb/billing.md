# Billing & Payments

## Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise plans only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise plans only)

## Invoices

### Viewing Invoices
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Invoices
2. View all past invoices with dates and amounts
3. Click any invoice to see full details

### Downloading Invoices
- Click the download icon next to any invoice
- Invoices are available in PDF format
- Receipts are sent automatically via email after each payment

## Billing Cycles
- Monthly plans are billed on the same day each month
- Annual plans are billed once per year on your signup anniversary
- You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time

## Refund Policy

### Monthly Plans
- Cancel within 7 days of signup for a full refund
- No partial refunds for mid-cycle cancellations
- Your access continues until the end of your billing period

### Annual Plans
- Cancel within 30 days for a full refund
- After 30 days, receive prorated credit for remaining months
- Credits can be applied to future purchases or refunded

### How to Request a Refund
1. Go to Settings > Billing > Request Refund
2. Select the transaction
3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Submit request
5. Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails:
1. We'll notify you via email
2. You have 7 days to update your payment method
3. We'll retry the payment automatically
4. After 3 failed attempts, your account may be suspended

## Upgrading and Downgrading

### Upgrading Your Plan
- Upgrades take effect immediately
- You'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle
- All features of the new plan are available instantly

### Downgrading Your Plan
- Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle
- You keep your current features until then
- Any features not included in your new plan will be disabled

## Tax Information
- Prices shown are exclusive of applicable taxes
- VAT is added for EU customers
- Sales tax is added for US customers where applicable
- Tax-exempt organizations can submit documentation in Settings > Billing
