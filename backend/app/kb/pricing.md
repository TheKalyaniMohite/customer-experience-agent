# Pricing Plans

## Basic Plan - $29/month
- Up to 100 customers
- Email support
- Basic analytics
- 1 team member
- Best for: Freelancers and solo entrepreneurs

## Pro Plan - $79/month
- Up to 1,000 customers
- Priority email + chat support
- Advanced analytics
- 5 team members
- API access
- Best for: Growing businesses and small teams

## Enterprise Plan - $199/month
- Unlimited customers
- 24/7 phone + email + chat support
- Custom analytics
- Unlimited team members
- Full API access
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations
- SSO and advanced security
- Best for: Large organizations with complex needs

## Free Trial
All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time during the trial.

## Discounts

### Annual Billing Discount
Save 20% when you pay annually instead of monthly. Annual plans are billed once per year.

### Student Discount
Students and educators get 50% off any plan! To qualify:
1. Must have a valid .edu email address
2. Submit proof of enrollment or faculty ID
3. Discount applies for the duration of your studies
4. Apply at: Settings > Billing > Student Discount

### Non-Profit Discount
Registered non-profit organizations receive 50% off all plans. Provide your 501(c)(3) documentation to qualify.

### Startup Discount
Startups less than 2 years old get 30% off for their first year. Must provide proof of incorporation date.
